Rarefied Gas Dynamics

Rarefied Gas Dynamics is the study of fluid flows where the density is too low for it to be approximated using continuum techniques

  • Low-pressure applications: vacuum, space
  • Micro/nano systems:

Droplet (interfacial) dynamics

Droplet dynamics is the study of droplet related phenomena, such as the coalescence, breakup, etc etc

  • Nucleation and phase change (boiling, cavitation, icing)
  • Droplet technology (PCR in droplet, lab-on-a-chip)
  • Drag reduction

Porous media flows

Porous media flows is the study of stuff with holes in it, like a leaky bucket

  • CO2 injection & storage
  • Shale gas extraction
