PhD Scholarships

We regularly have EU/UK PhD scholarships to award. To be eligible, you need to have a first class degree (or equivalent) honours engineering degree (or a degree in a relevant subject area, such as in applied maths, informatics, or computer science) and have a strong background in mathematics, physics, and computer programming. For candidates who have not yet graduated, transcript evidence of likely degree level will be sufficient. In the first instance, please email a full Curriculum Vitae and cover letter to us at the email address above, explaining why you are interested in pursuing a PhD in our research group, with the names and contact details of two referees. Our scholarships are fully-funded for UK or EU students, which cover University fees and a tax-free maintenance grant. Other overseas students who apply for a PhD will need to show proof they can provide the difference in the university fees.

Research Associate Vacancies
