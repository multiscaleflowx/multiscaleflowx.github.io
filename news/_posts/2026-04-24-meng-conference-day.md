Christiana Antonopoulou won the best presentation prize for her session at the Mechanical Engineering MEng conference day on 24 April 2026, for her project on interfacial thermal transport. Angus Stainton finished as runner-up in his session for his project on vibration-assisted freeze desalination. Both are final-year MEng students supervised by [Rohit].
